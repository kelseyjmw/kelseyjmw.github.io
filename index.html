Featured Project

The Island of Enoki

Currently featured on my website is my second animal crossing island, Enoki— a mysterious island full of greenery and old ruins. What kind of civilization used to exist here? Pictured to the left is the entrance to Enoki from the shore. Mysterious robotic creatures sing in the forest, bathed in warm sunlight. ✿

Warm Welcome

As you walk into the strange forest, beams of light break through the trees as the pleasant song of the forest spirits welcome you. This place is unlike any regular forest– yet strangely enough, it feels like home. ✿

Strange Sights

One of the most mysterious sights found on the island of Enoki is a large school, who's garden thrives despite no signs of humans inhabiting this island for many years. Some say the animals who inhabit Enoki are really forest spirits from another world, and that they care for the gardens here.✿

A Civilization Lost to Time

Enoki island is filled with lush forests and waterfalls. Reminants from a past civilization can be seen poking through the trees as you explore this island. It is said that if you visit Enoki at the right time, you may be transported to a time long ago, when the island was booming with life— but this is just an old story. If you want to find out the truth, perhaps you should take a visit yourself?✿
