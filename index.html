Pokemon Search

Welcome to OakDB! Use the search bar and Type filters to find your favorite Pokemon. View the full details of each Pokemon by clicking on the "Full Details" button on each Pokemon's card. You may also save your favorite Pokemon by clicking "Favorite" on each Pokemon's card!

OakDB is a project developed by Kelsey White for CSCI 2170: Server Side Scripting at Dalhousie University.
