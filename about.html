Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe:
Type Ingredient Measurement
Dry White Flour 1¼ cups
Baking Soda ½ tsp
Salt ½ tsp
Cream Brown Sugar ¼ cups
White Sugar ½ cup
Unsalted Margarine ½ cup
Liquid Egg 1
Vanilla Extract 2 tsp
1 Cup Chocolate Chips

Directions:

  1. Preheat Oven to 350℉.
  2. Cream together brown sugar, white sugar, and margarine in a large bowl.
  3. In another bowl, beat egg and vanilla together.
  4. Combine liquid ingredients with creamed ingredients until evenly mixed.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
  6. Slowly mix dry ingredients with the previously combined ingredients.
  7. Once evenly mixed, carefully fold in chocolate chips.
  8. Bake at 350℉ for 10-15 minutes.
  9. *Optional* Add extra chocolate chips and a pinch of salt on top of the cookies about 2 minutes before they're finished baking.
  10. Let cool for 2-5 minutes, then enjoy!
